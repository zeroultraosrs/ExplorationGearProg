Why is 98 agility on the PVM chart?

Hallowed Sepulchre is uniquely strong for ironman progression, yielding much more than just agility xp. Assuming you 98 agility anyways, sepulchre is very strong in the early game, netting tons of resources and exp in other skills, enabling other powerful unlocks early game.

Raw coins and runes

Sepulchre sources important and expensive runes. A large portion of the law rune stack can even comfortably be sold for significant gp.

  • 24.5m gp
  • 44.7k blood runes (17.9m gp cost)
  • 44.7k soul runes (15.0m gp cost)
  • 44.7k law runes (8.9m gp cost, 5.4m GAINED if sold)
  • 8.1k death runes (1.6m gp cost)
  • 6.1k cosmic runes (340k gp cost)
Total: 64.8m coins. (~81 hours of air battlestaves, 5.6m crafting)

Experience

Sepulchre levels not only agility, but also thieving, construction, and magic. Furthermore, it provides near ideal conditions to also multiskill fletching and alchs.

  • 565k Thieving XP
  • 444k Construction XP
  • ~600k Fletching XP (headless arrow / broad arrow, multiskilled)
  • ~3.2m Magic XP (Alchs, multiskilled)

    • Cost of skipping

    82-98 agility is a 129 hour grind which by conventional standards is pretty long.
