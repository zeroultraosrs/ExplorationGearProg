Legend

It is expected that you don't necessarily obtain every item on this route when they appear. Red denotes optional items, yellow denotes items you can delay until later, and blue denotes long term grinds.
You can mark items as skipped by right clicking them.

Text Version

This chart is based on the Ironman PvM Exploration Guide. Which contains routing for bosses that don't have meaningful gear unlocks, alongside more thorough explanations for overall progression and specific steps. It also currently houses the FAQ, as most questions aren't frequent enough to put them on this website.
